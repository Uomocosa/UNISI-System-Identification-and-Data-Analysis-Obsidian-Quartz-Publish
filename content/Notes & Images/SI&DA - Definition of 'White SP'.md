Given a **White SP**:
$$
e(t) \sim \operatorname{WP}(0, \sigma_e^2)
$$ We can say that:
$$
\begin{array}{l}
m_e = 0
\\
R_e(\tau) = 
	\left\{\begin{array}{l}
	R(0) = \sigma_e^2 &\tau =0 
	\\
	0 & \tau \neq 0
	\end{array}\right.
\end{array}
$$
So:
$$
R_e(\tau) = \sigma_e^2 \cdot \delta(\tau)
$$
